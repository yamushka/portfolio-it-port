About Me

About Joanna Jodel

I’m a developer who values clarity — in design, in code, and in how people use the web; with a background in recruitment, I switched careers to build things myself.

Now I focus on developing web apps that are structured, accessible, and simple to use. I like minimal design and clean logic. I care more about building things right than building them fast. I like systems that make sense, interfaces that feel calm, and websites that respect your time.

I’m a visual thinker. I need to understand how things connect, and I’ll keep working on something until it fits — functionally and visually.

  • – I don’t like clutter. In code, in design, or in my apartment.
  • – I will refactor a function until it stops annoying me.
  • – If it works but it’s ugly, I will rebuild it.
  • – The button doesn’t need to spin. It needs to work.
